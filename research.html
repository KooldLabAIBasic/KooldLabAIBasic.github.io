What We're Building

We apply frontier AI to physical and engineered systems — communications, signal processing, robotics, and control. Research publications are in progress; our first products are under active development.

Edge AI Products — Under Development

We are actively building AI systems designed to run at the edge — close to the physical layer, where latency and compute constraints are real. Our current focus areas span intelligent wireless transceivers, on-device signal processing, and lightweight neural architectures for embedded systems.

These are not demos. They are engineering-grade implementations built to the standards we believe the field requires.
