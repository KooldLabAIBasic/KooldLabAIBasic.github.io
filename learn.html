Telecom AI: Neural Networks for Wireless Transceivers

A comprehensive, engineering-first course on applying neural networks to wireless communications. You'll learn how to replace or augment traditional transceiver blocks with learned counterparts — from neural channel estimation and equalization to end-to-end autoencoder designs.

The course is designed for engineers and researchers who already have a grounding in either communications systems or machine learning, and want to bridge the two fields with hands-on PyTorch implementations and reproducible code.

What you'll learn

  • ✓ OFDM systems & channel models
  • ✓ Neural channel estimation
  • ✓ Deep learning equalization
  • ✓ End-to-end autoencoder design
  • ✓ PyTorch implementations
  • ✓ Reproducible experiments
